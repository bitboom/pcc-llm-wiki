# Apple PCC VRE Docs

## Metadata

- Publisher: Apple Security Research
- Date: 2024-10-24
- URL: https://security.apple.com/documentation/private-cloud-compute/virtualresearchenvironment
- Raw source metadata: tracked in the repository under `raw/`.
## Key claims

- Apple exposes a documented path for researchers to inspect releases and run a virtualized PCC environment on Apple silicon.
- The VRE turns transparency from a passive publication model into an active inspection workflow.
- The existence of setup documentation lowers the barrier to independent validation compared with opaque server-side systems.

## Impact on the wiki

- Supports the claim that Apple’s auditability is operational, not only conceptual.
- Feeds the VRE concept page and the auditability comparison page.
